URL Slugs with Node.js & MongoDB

Today I added semantic urls to my blog posts and wanted to take a few minutes to document the process and how I set this up here on the site. You can find the code I used located on my GitHub. I also found this post by Ben McMahen very helpful, and borrowed part of his functions for my implementation. He references some interesting details about the history of how URL Slugs got their name from the publishing industry which got me to thinking, a bit nostalically, back to my days of designing the daily paper layouts working at the Red & Black when I was in college.

More on those days in a later non-topical post :). For me, URL slugs were a bit confusing before I had enough experience with Mongo lookups and passing 'ids' through routes.
